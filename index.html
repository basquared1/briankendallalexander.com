About

Practical counsel, creative-friendly execution.

I partner with creative, production, and business teams to structure and close deals that are clear, durable, and executable, from first conversations through signatures and delivery.

My background spans production companies, networks/distributors, and studios/funders, which helps me anticipate friction points early and keep negotiations moving without losing what matters.

How I work

Low drama. High clarity. Forward motion.

I aim to be the person who makes it easier for creative and business teams to say “yes”, with terms that are aligned, defensible, and workable in the real world.

I’m direct, collaborative, and disciplined about getting to the key business points early, so negotiations don’t drift into endless paper.

Recent Accomplishments at UMG

As Senior Vice President of Business & Legal Affairs at UMG’s Polygram Entertainment, I led all film and television dealmaking for eight years, helping build the division into a meaningful player in premium documentary and music-driven content.
