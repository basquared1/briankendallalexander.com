About

Practical counsel, creative-friendly execution.

I partner with creative, production, and business teams to structure and close deals that are clear, durable, and executable, from first conversations through signatures and delivery.

My background spans production companies, networks/distributors, and studios/funders, which helps me anticipate friction points early and keep negotiations moving without losing what matters.

How I work

Low drama. High clarity. Forward motion.

Recent accomplishments

UMG / Polygram Entertainment

As Senior Vice President of Business & Legal Affairs,over an eight-year period, Polygram Entertainment was transformed from a nascent initiative into a globally recognized film and television studio within Universal Music Group, the world’s largest music company. During that time, I led business and legal affairs across a rapidly expanding slate that spanned premium documentaries, scripted features, and television, helping to build the operational and dealmaking infrastructure that enabled the company’s growth. The studio delivered a steady stream of high-profile, culturally impactful projects with top creative partners including Ron Howard, Spike Jonze, Morgan Neville, and Todd Haynes, and platforms such as Netflix, HBO, Apple TV+, NBC, and Amazon. Its work has been widely recognized with major industry honors, including Emmy Awards and nominations across multiple titles, a Peabody Award for Stax: Soulsville U.S.A., and festival recognition including the Audience Award at Sundance for Selena y Los Dinos. What began as a catalog-driven experiment evolved into a scalable, multi-platform content business, demonstrating how world-class IP, creative ideas, disciplined execution, and strategic talent and platform partnerships can generate enduring franchise value.

Noah Kahan: Out of Body / Netflix Selena y Los Dinos / Netflix Man on the Run: A Paul McCartney Story / Amazon The Bee Gees: How Can You Mend a Broken Heart / HBO The Velvet Underground / Apple

I structured and closed first look agreements, complex rights and distribution deals, and helped establish Polygram as a premium content partner to Netflix, Amazon, HBO, Apple, NBC, and others.
